2. Recommendation Scoring Specification

ComponentWeight
Disease Safety40%
Nutrition Budget Fit25%
Goal Alignment20%
User Preference10%
Diet Variety5%
FinalScore =
DiseaseSafetyScore * 0.40 +
BudgetFitScore * 0.25 +
GoalFitScore * 0.20 +
PreferenceScore * 0.10 +
VarietyScore * 0.05

CRITICAL violations are hard filtered before scoring.
